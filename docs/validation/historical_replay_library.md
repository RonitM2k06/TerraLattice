# Historical Replay Candidate Library

This library catalogs critical historical events that will serve as ground-truth for testing TerraLattice's "Historical Replay" mechanics, validating edge strengths, and calibrating the digital twin.

## 1. 1970s Sahel Drought
*   **Climate Shock:** Severe, multi-year rainfall deficit (Drought Index $\uparrow$, Rainfall $\downarrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** Complete failure of rain-fed agriculture; catastrophic loss of pastoral herds (Crop Yield $\downarrow$, Livestock Health $\downarrow$).
*   **Economic Impact:** Massive inflation in local food markets (Food Prices $\uparrow$).
*   **Social Impact:** Mass displacement and nomadic migration southward; extreme malnutrition (Migration $\uparrow$, Malnutrition Risk $\uparrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Overwhelmed local governments; heavy reliance on international Disaster Relief.

## 2. 2010 Russian Heatwave
*   **Climate Shock:** Persistent blocking high causing extreme temperatures and fires (Temperature $\uparrow$, Wildfire Probability $\uparrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** Devastation of the wheat harvest (Crop Yield $\downarrow$).
*   **Economic Impact:** Global wheat price spike (Food Prices $\uparrow$).
*   **Social Impact:** Food riots in importing countries (e.g., contributing to Arab Spring dynamics) (Social Stress $\uparrow$, Political Stability $\downarrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Russia implemented export bans (Import Controls $\uparrow$), exacerbating the global price shock.

## 3. Horn of Africa Drought (Multiple, e.g., 2011)
*   **Climate Shock:** Consecutive failed rainy seasons (Rainfall $\downarrow$, Water Stress $\uparrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** Widespread crop failure and livestock death (Water Availability $\downarrow$).
*   **Economic Impact:** Localized economic collapse and soaring staple food prices.
*   **Social Impact:** Famine conditions, massive refugee movements into neighboring countries (e.g., Kenya) (Poverty $\uparrow$, Migration $\uparrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Activation of global humanitarian aid networks; regional instability requiring external intervention.

## 4. 2019–2020 Australian Bushfires
*   **Climate Shock:** Extreme heat and prolonged drought culminating in unprecedented fire weather (Wildfire Probability $\uparrow$, Temperature $\uparrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** Destruction of farmland, livestock, and local ecosystems.
*   **Economic Impact:** Billions in damages, disruption to tourism, and localized GDP impact (GDP $\downarrow$).
*   **Social Impact:** Mass evacuations, extreme air quality degradation causing acute Health System Stress (Heat/Smoke Mortality $\uparrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Major inquiries into disaster management, shifts in carbon policy discourse.

## 5. 2022 Pakistan Floods
*   **Climate Shock:** Exceptional monsoon rains amplified by glacial melt (Rainfall $\uparrow$, Flood Risk $\uparrow$, River Flow $\uparrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** One-third of the country submerged; massive loss of cotton and wheat crops (Crop Yield $\downarrow$).
*   **Economic Impact:** >$30 billion in damages, severe inflation, sovereign debt crisis amplification (GDP $\downarrow$, Inflation $\uparrow$).
*   **Social Impact:** 33 million people displaced, outbreak of waterborne diseases (Migration $\uparrow$, Disease Risk $\uparrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Pleas for international debt relief and climate reparations (Loss and Damage funds).

## 6. European Heatwave 2022
*   **Climate Shock:** Record-breaking summer temperatures and extreme drought (Temperature $\uparrow$, Drought Index $\uparrow$, River Flow $\downarrow$).
*   **Agricultural Impact:** Reduced yields for maize and sunflower; severe Water Stress impacting irrigation.
*   **Economic Impact:** Energy crisis compounded by low river levels hindering transport and nuclear plant cooling; inflation spike.
*   **Social Impact:** Significant excess heat-related deaths (Heat Mortality $\uparrow$).
*   **Policy Impact:** Implementation of strict water usage controls across municipalities, acceleration of renewable energy investments.
